# VoiceCraft Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-06

VoiceCraft is a voice chat client for connecting to VoiceCraft-compatible servers selected by the user.

## Data We Process

VoiceCraft stores app settings locally on your device, including server profiles, audio settings, theme settings, and generated identifiers used by the VoiceCraft protocol.

When you join a server, VoiceCraft sends the audio and connection data needed to provide realtime voice chat to that server. VoiceCraft does not record or retain your voice chat audio.

## Optional Telemetry

VoiceCraft can send anonymous telemetry after you consent in the app. This telemetry may include app version, platform, operating system and runtime information, architecture, locale, CPU and memory information, startup diagnostics, crash data, performance data, and other technical diagnostics.

Crash dumps are uploaded only when you explicitly use the dump link action in the Crash Logs screen.

Telemetry is sent to `https://vc-api.avion.team`.

## Data We Do Not Collect

VoiceCraft does not collect contacts, location, photos, videos, payment information, health or fitness data, browsing history, search history, or advertising data.

VoiceCraft does not use telemetry for advertising tracking and does not sell personal data.

## Your Choices

You can decline telemetry during the first launch consent prompt. You can also enable or disable anonymous telemetry later in VoiceCraft settings.

You can remove local VoiceCraft data by deleting the app from your device. Server operators may have their own privacy practices for servers you choose to connect to.
