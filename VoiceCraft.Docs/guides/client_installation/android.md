# Downloading

Go to the [Latest Release](https://github.com/AvionBlock/VoiceCraft/releases/latest) page and download the file named
`VoiceCraft.Client.Android.<Architecture>.apk`.

The \<Architecture\> to choose and download needs to be the architecture of your CPU, the most common one is arm64.

# Installing

Go to where you have downloaded the `VoiceCraft.Client.Android.<Architecture>.apk` file in your file manager and open 
the file. This will open the android app installer (which may vary between devices and OS's) and click on the `install` 
button. If a Google play protect screen comes up, you can click on `More Info` and click on `Install Anyways` 
(wording may vary).

# Launching

You can just open the app like any normal mobile application in your home screen.
